About Us

This web application was first created by the students of K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering as part of a final year project under the guidance of faculty of IT Department in the academic year 2021-2022.

We at rE-Learn promote and empower regional language learning for all our users and stand by the ideaology that Language is a vital part of human connection and our prime goal is to we bridge the gap between traditional and self-learning methods using an AI-enabled language learning platform.
